May Monthly Competition

What is the Monthly Competition about? Each month our discord members take an optional survey where they post data from their current badges, Pokemon, and achievements. This page is designed to be able to show those filters and provide a quick way to see where you stack up amongst your fellow trainers. Now go play and see if you can top the leaderboards!
