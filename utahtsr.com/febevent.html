February Community Day Recap

We learned from our mistakes in the January Community Day Event by spreading the locations sooner, boosting our server capacity, and reaching out to the community to come and receive their Shiny Dratini badge. Although we had some issues with the website right as the event was beginning, we were able to quickly get it up and running and the check-in process was smoother. Along with that we enabled the feature where when you check-in with an Event Staff it automatically did a handshake at the same time for your unique traveler card.
