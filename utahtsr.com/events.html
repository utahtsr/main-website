November Event Information

What is #PokemonGOCommunityDay? For just a few hours each month, Trainers can encounter a special Pokémon in the wild. During these hours, there's a chance to learn a previously unavailable move for that Pokémon, as well as earn some Community Day bonuses. Celebrate what it means to be a part of the Pokémon GO community and make new friends along the way!

Want to join the network? Claim your public traveler card before community day. Create your traveler card here!

Event Details
  • Cyndaquil
  • 10 November 2018
  • 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM MST
  • 2x Catch XP
  • 2x Catch Stardust
  • 3 hour Lures
  • Exclusive Move: Blast Burn

In addition to the bonuses above, local representatives from The Silph Road will be in attendance to hand out digital badges for your Silph Trainer Cards. Check out the event details and we hope to see you there!

Note: It is your responsibility to reach out and find your community day event host during the event time frame. Digital badges cannot be given to those who fail to find their event host during the event.

NEW INFO: The Silph Road App is official for iOS and Android. Use the app to get checked in for a faster and smoother process! p.s. Sorry about any delays for iOS, it is still a work in progress getting approval through the Apple App Store.
